\section{Conclusion}

We introduced \taskname, a slide-aware contextual SST setting motivated by real lectures and scientific talks.  Rather than treating context as always helpful, we formulated slides, glossary entries, and background documents as heterogeneous evidence that must be selected and trusted under streaming constraints.  The proposed \datasetname{} benchmark and \policyname{} context manager provide a minimal testbed for studying when slides help, when they hurt, and how SST systems can use deployment context without over-committing to wrong evidence.
